# K9 ProTrain - Subscription Tiers & Features

> Last Updated: January 18, 2026

---

## Overview

K9 ProTrain offers two account types with multiple subscription tiers:

1. **Family (Dog Parent)** - For pet owners working with trainers
2. **Business** - For dog trainers, boarding facilities, and training businesses

All paid plans include a **14-day free trial**. Annual billing saves **15%**.
